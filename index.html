Wenxuan Wang
Hi! I am currently an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science, Northwestern Polytechnical University.
[2022-2023] I was a senior researcher at Intel Asia-Pacific Research & Development Ltd.
[2017-2022] I received Ph.D. from the Fudan University, under the supervision of Prof. Xiangyang Xue.
[2013-2017] I received my Bachelor's degree from the School of Computer Science in Northwestern Polytechnical University.
At present, I focus on studying trusty AI and human understanding with deep learning.
More details can be found in my CV. Most publications can be found at Google Scholar.
