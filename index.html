Wenxuan Wang
Hi! I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student at Fudan University, under the supervision of Prof. Yanwei Fu and Prof. Xiangyang Xue.
I received my Bachelor's degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2017, and majored in Computer Science and Technology during my undergraduate study.
At present, I devote to the research of adversarial attack, generative adversarial networks, person re-identification, face recognition, attribute classification with deep learning.
More details can be found in my CV. Most publications can be found at Google Scholar.
