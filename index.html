Wenxuan Wang
Hi! I am currently an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science, Northwestern Polytechnical University. I was a senior researcher at Intel Asia-Pacific Research & Development Ltd. I received Ph.D. from the Fudan University, under the supervision of Prof. Yanwei Fu and Prof. Xiangyang Xue, and received my Bachelor's degree from the School of Computer Science at Northwestern Polytechnical University. At present, I focus on studying trusty AI and human understanding with deep learning. Most publications can be found at Google Scholar.
